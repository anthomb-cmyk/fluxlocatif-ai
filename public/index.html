FAQ

Common questions

Is the monthly fee a setup fee?

No. The monthly membership covers the active leasing support for your unit or units during the month.

Do I pay extra when a unit gets rented?

No. The core pricing model is based on monthly memberships. The membership covers the leasing work itself.

What does the membership include?

Each membership includes inquiry management, tenant prequalification, applicant organization, AI-assisted matching, and ongoing leasing support. Choose your plan based on how many active units you want us to handle.
